A Cheesy Poem

I love the way you make me smile,
like warm cheese melting for a while.
Soft and rich, a little bold-
comfort better than cheddar gold.

Some days you're sharp like aged brie,
some days calm like creamy brie-
wait, that rhyme went slightly wrong,
but you still feel like my favorite song.

If love were measured wedge by wedge,
I'd stack it high right to the edge.
Because between the cheese… and you-
I'd still pick you.

...Most of the time.

- HallMatthew
